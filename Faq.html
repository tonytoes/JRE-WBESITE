What is JRE ?

JRE stands for Jonrose Reithan Eltopia, is a company that focuses on selling customizable clothing and accessories. Inspired by family values, JRE aims to provide stylish and unique products while adapting to modern shopping trends like e-commerce.

Can I cancel my order ?

Unfortunately, our current system does not support order cancellation directly on the website. However, you can contact us via email or phone for further assistance. While we cannot guarantee cancellation after the order has been processed, we will do our best to assist you based on the order status

Can I return or exchange items ?

If you need to return or exchange an item, please contact our support team with your order number and reason for the request. Once approved, further instructions will be provided to ensure a smooth process.

What payment methods do you accept ?

At the moment, JRE only accepts cash payments. Since our website does not currently support online payment options, all transactions will be completed in person or via cash-on-delivery. We are working to expand our payment methods in the future to include secure online payment systems for your convenience.

What are your operating hours ?

Our online store is open for browsing and purchasing from Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM, and is closed on Sundays. During operating hours, you can contact us via phone or email for inquiries, assistance with orders, or feedback. We aim to respond to all inquiries promptly to ensure an excellent customer experience.

What sizes do you offer ?

We provide a wide range of sizes for our clothing products. Detailed size charts are available on each product page, including measurements for chest, waist, and length, to help you select the best fit.

Can I create an account on the JRE website ?

Currently, the JRE website does not support account creation or login systems. All purchases can be made as a guest. In future updates, we aim to introduce user accounts for order tracking and personalization.

Is the JRE website mobile-friendly ?

While the website is optimized for desktop browsing, some mobile-responsive features may still be limited. We recommend accessing the site on a desktop or laptop for the best experience.

What happens if my order arrives damaged ?

If your order is damaged upon arrival, please contact our support team within 48 hours. Provide your order number, photos of the damaged item, and a description of the issue. We will evaluate your case and, if eligible, offer a replacement or refund
